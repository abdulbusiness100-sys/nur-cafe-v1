## App Description

Nur Cafe brings the warmth of authentic Arabic and Lebanese hospitality straight to your pocket. Skip the queue, explore our handcrafted menu, and have your order waiting for you the moment you walk through the door.

Born in the heart of Manchester, Nur is more than a cafe. Every cup of cardamom-spiced coffee, every freshly baked manoushe, every glass of house-blended lemonade carries a tradition of care that stretches back generations. Now, with the Nur Cafe app, that same care extends to how you order.

BROWSE THE FULL MENU
Scroll through our curated selection of specialty Arabic coffees, loose-leaf teas, fresh juices, smoothies, pastries, and savoury plates. High-resolution photos and detailed descriptions help you discover new favourites or find your usual in seconds.

ORDER AHEAD
Place your order before you leave the house, the office, or the lecture hall. Choose your pickup time, and we will have everything prepared fresh when you arrive. No waiting, no rushing.

PAY SEAMLESSLY
Secure checkout powered by Stripe. Add your card once, then pay with a single tap on every future visit. Apple Pay supported for the fastest experience possible.

NUR REWARDS
Every order earns you points towards free drinks, pastries, and exclusive menu items. The more you visit, the more you unlock. It is our way of saying thank you for making Nur part of your routine.

TRACK YOUR ORDER
Watch your order move from received to preparation to ready for pickup. Real-time status updates mean you always know exactly when to head in.

PERSONALISED FOR YOU
The app remembers your favourites, your dietary preferences, and your order history. Reorder your regular in two taps, or let our recommendations introduce you to something new.

BUILT FOR MANCHESTER
Nur Cafe is rooted in this city. Our app is designed for the pace of Manchester life, whether you are grabbing a quick Turkish coffee before a meeting in Spinningfields, ordering a family-sized platter for a weekend gathering, or treating yourself to a quiet afternoon with a pot of Moroccan mint tea.

Download Nur Cafe and experience the hospitality you deserve, every single visit.
